Platform Quality & Reliability Program

A 160-day structured initiative to eliminate root-cause failures, enforce quality standards, clarify ownership, and bring Tableau workbook performance within acceptable thresholds across all CBRE clients.

Program Leadership

  • John Hernandez — Program Lead, Monitoring & Alerting, Tableau Performance
  • Megan Lane — Story / QA Redesign
  • Chris Jack — Operating Model Alignment
  • Dan Hernandez — Recurring Issue Elimination

Program Timeline

  • Start: April 13, 2026
  • Phase 1 End: May 4, 2026
  • Phase 2 End: May 25, 2026
  • Phase 3 End: July 7, 2026
  • Final Target: September 30, 2026

Success Criteria

  • Managed Services issues reduced to ≤50/month
  • 90% of Olympic JIRA stories meet QA standards
  • 90% reduction in highest-volume recurring failures
  • All priority Tableau workbooks under 8-second load time

Guiding Principles

  • Alerts only fire when human action is required
  • Every failure has a named owner and an SLA
  • QA is embedded in delivery, not bolted on after
  • Permanent fixes, not repeated incident response
"We don't win by responding faster to the same problems — we win by making them impossible to repeat."— PQRP Program Charter
